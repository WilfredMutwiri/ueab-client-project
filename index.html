~~ About Us ~~

The Department of Information Systems and Computing offers specialized programs in Software Engineering, Networking and Communication Systems, and Business Information Technology, as well as a diploma program in Information Communication Technology. It provides a Christian education, equipping students with technological knowledge, technical expertise, and professionalism. The department partners with Huawei for industry certifications and competitions.

~~ More Concerning us ~~

The Department of Information Systems and Computing offers specialized degree programs in Software Engineering, Networking and Communication Systems, and Business Information Technology, as well as a comprehensive diploma program in Information Communication Technology. The department recognizes the importance of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in today's interconnected world. The department provides a comprehensive Christian education, equipping students with cutting-edge technological knowledge, technical expertise, social competence, and professionalism. The distinguished faculty, who specialize in various fields, provide students with a broad foundation of understanding and are actively engaged in research and development. The department has a strategic partnership with HUAWEI, a renowned technology leader, allowing students to pursue industry-recognized certifications and participate in Huawei competitions. The department also nurtures students' character and moral fortitude, preparing them to make a positive impact in their chosen careers.
